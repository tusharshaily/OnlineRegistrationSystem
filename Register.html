E-SUMMIT

E-Summit is the annual flagship event of E-Cell , MMMUT , to foster entrepreneurial initiatives and activities in the campus. E-Summit provides a platform for the ‘aspiring entrepreneurs’ to have hands-on experience before they can finally enter the market. It brings together the academic community, venture capitalists, new age entrepreneurs and all those passionate about entrepreneurship to a common ground.

This event provides an avenue to exhibit their entrepreneurial talents, creativity, innovation through various associated happenings

UXD CLASSES

Entrepreneurship Development Cell of MMMUT conduct evening classes on the theme “Design and User Experience as a major role in Start-ups”. The classes will be free of cost and open to all the students of the university. The program will be focusing on direct practical approach of UX Design including hands-on practice on software applications (Photoshop, Illustrator, PowerPoint and Aftereffects) which helps in designing. On completion of the classes the students will be awarded with the certificates of introductory course completion in UX Design from Entrepreneurship Development Cell.

WORKSHOPS & SEMINAR

These workshops usually feature an experienced entrepreneur-practitioner as a guest speaker and allow for learning key entrepreneurial concepts and practices and for networking with like-minded people.

E-Cell organizes various seminars and speaking sessions regularly which provide expert advice, insight and guidance to those who wish to start and scale their own ventures and make them aware of the emerging trend of startups.

These are inspirational and motivational sessions, featuring high profile guests who share their experiences, opinions and advice in a chat show format. Also, the students are informed about the prerequisites of startups and solutions are given to their queries
